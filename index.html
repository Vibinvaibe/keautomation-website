Krishna Electrical & Automation is a trusted name in steel fabrication, electrical manufacturing, and electrical trading. OUR SERVICES

We specialize in in-house fabrication of heavy and light steel structures required for industrial and power projects. Our manufacturing capabilities allow us to maintain quality, timelines, and customization as per project requirements.

Lighting Poles
We manufacture lighting poles as per custom height, load, and site requirements, compatible with various fixtures and luminaire types. Ideal for solar plants, industrial areas, highways, and infrastructure projects.
Foundation Bolts
We manufacture L Bolts, J Bolts, Anchor Bolts, and Custom Foundation Bolts as per drawings and specifications. Designed to provide strong structural anchoring for poles, towers, and heavy steel structures.
Cable Tray & Cable Tray Supports
We manufacture robust cable tray supports and structural accessories to ensure safe routing and long-term cable management. Built to handle heavy electrical loads and suitable for industrial, solar, and infrastructure installations.
Industrial & Solar Structures
We fabricate custom steel structures for industrial plants and solar projects based on load calculations and site conditions. Our structures ensure durability, stability, and efficient installation at project sites.
Fencing & Security Structures
We manufacture industrial fencing, WMS fencing, and security structures for controlled access and site protection. Commonly used in solar plants, factories, warehouses, and restricted areas.
Custom Steel Fabrication
We offer drawing-based custom steel fabrication, tailored to project-specific requirements and applications. From one-off components to bulk orders, we deliver precision, strength, and consistency.
Electrical Equipment & Supplies
Along with fabrication, we supply a wide range of electrical equipment, tools, and accessories required for installation and maintenance. From cables and cable trays to crimping tools and hardware, we provide complete electrical sourcing support.
